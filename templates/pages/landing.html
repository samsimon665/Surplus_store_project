Frequently Asked Questions

How does weight-based pricing work? expand_more

You pay a flat rate per kilogram for all clothing items in your cart. The total price is calculated at checkout based on the combined weight of your selected items. It's a simple, transparent way to get more for your money.

What is "surplus" clothing? expand_more

Surplus clothing is brand-new apparel that comes from overproduction, canceled orders, or past-season stock from major retailers. It's high-quality clothing that we rescue and offer to you at a significant discount.

What is your return policy? expand_more

We offer a 14-day return policy on all items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or store credit. Please see our Returns & Refunds page for full details.
