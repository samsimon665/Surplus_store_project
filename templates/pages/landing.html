F.A.Q.

HOW DOES WEIGHT‑BASED PRICING WORK? expand_more
A flat rate per kilogram applies across surplus clothing in your cart, with the total calculated from the combined weight of all items at checkout.
WHAT IS "SURPLUS" CLOTHING? expand_more
Surplus items are new garments from overproduction, canceled orders or previous seasons that are redirected from traditional retail channels into a second‑chance inventory.
WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY? expand_more
Returns are accepted within 14 days, with options for a full refund or store credit depending on your preference and the condition of items when they are received back.
